🏛️ ECI Official Guidelines

How to fill the Special Intensive Revision (SIR) Form:

  • EPIC Mismatch: Copy the exact EPIC number printed on your BLO slip, even if it has a typo.
  • 2002 Data: This is critical for verification. If your family migrated after 2002, provide the oldest registered member's details.
  • Photo Rules: The photo must have a plain white background, facing forward, with no sunglasses or hats. Our AI handles the background for you!
  • Original Form: This PDF is a Reference Copy. You MUST write these exact details using a blue ballpoint pen onto the physical QR-coded form given by your BLO.
