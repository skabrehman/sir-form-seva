🏛️ Official ECI Guidelines

How to fill the SIR (Special Intensive Revision) Form based on Govt rules:

  • Original Form is Mandatory: This PDF is a Reference Copy to make your life easier. You MUST write these exact details onto the physical QR-coded form given by your BLO using a blue pen.
  • EPIC Mismatch: Copy the exact EPIC number printed on your BLO slip, even if it has a typo.
  • 2002 Data: This is critical for verification. If your family migrated after 2002, provide the oldest registered member's details.
  • Photo Rules: The photo must have a plain white background, facing forward. Our AI handles the background removal for you!
